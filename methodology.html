Methodology

How CPI Works

The California Polo Index estimates team strength using available major tournament results.

Public record policy

CPI does not show overall season record unless the dataset represents a complete season. Current pages show latest tracked major tournament record to avoid misleading users.

Club pages

Club pages show all ranked teams from that club. They do not list home pool because many clubs train at multiple facilities.

Trademark and logo note

Team and club names are used for identification. Club logos should only be added after verifying the official source and appropriate usage.

California Polo Index is independent and unofficial. Club names and future logos are used for identification only. Records shown are from the latest tracked major tournament, not complete season records.
